Study on Worldwide CO2 emissions¶

This Project analyses the change in CO2 emissions of 187 countries worldwide and tries to find possible trends in the CO2 emissions with parameters related to economy, human development and urbanisation.

The Project has been divided into Various Sections

  • Data Sets Used for the Project
  • Data Cleaning
  • Exploratory Data Analysis
  • Conclusions

Research Questions¶

  1. Have the CO2 emissions increased worldwide over the past decade?
  2. Does CO2 emissions depend on Economic Growth, Urbanizaton and Human Development?

Data Sets used for the projecct¶

  1. CO2 emissions- Total Carbon dioxide emissions due to burning of fossil fuels

  2. Total GDP in PPP - Gross Domestic Product converted into international dollars using purchasing power parity rates

  3. Human Development Index (HDI) - Index used to rank countries by levels of human development. It contains three dimensions: health level, educational level and living standard

  4. Urban Population- Refers to people living in urban areas as defined by national statistical offices
